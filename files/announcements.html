O5-5 11-Jun-21 05:32 PM
Area IX's future: Project Canary It seems like the community is still wondering what is underlying behind all these projects (Canary, Eclipse, Helios...), and as our goal is to be as transparent as possible, we will make sure to let you know what's going on behind the scenes. First off, you may have noticed an activity decrease. This phenomenon is congruent with this period of the year, as a lot of people are having their finals, or otherwise are getting overwhelmed by schoolwork after a year of Covid. Meanwhile, we are preparing a brand new site design under the name of Project Canary. We have already brought major changes to the genre with the release of V3, which also allowed the group to grow steeply like never before. We aim at reiterating such performance with something completely unique: > A way larger site with all adequate locations that are currently missing and advanced design > Including military and industrial infrastructures that were never seen in the genre > Organized around a lot of different floors, with plenty of elevators > Mostly open-plan, which means no prefab duplicated hallways (edited)
But we're not just remaking the site. In fact, a lot of development projects for departments were halted by the lack of a proper build infrastructure to work with. This site will allow these projects to come true. Some of you have already heard of the Catering Department and the Department of Technological Research, respectively part of Project Nothing (for once) and Project Eclipse. Catering Department The catering department will be tasked with making and serving food to all foundation personnel and Class D's at mealtimes. They might also receive janitorial duties. Might seem crazy but this will actually be interesting. We're going to make an advanced cooking system and a lot of realistic features.
